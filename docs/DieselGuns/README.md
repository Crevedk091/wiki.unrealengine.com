Overview
--------

"Diesel Guns" - is the old-school arcade car combat game like as Twisted Metal, interstate 76, Vigilante 8, San Francisco Rush 2049.

Gameplay
--------

Balanced weapons and map: every weapon is unique, and the player have to choose the right moment for each of them to waste the ammo more effectively and defeat the opponent. eSport futures: —Åontrol at the level of reflex, behavior forecast for opponent, equal opportunities. Stop and you will die. Speed gives bonuses to the rate of weapons, damage and speed for projectiles.
